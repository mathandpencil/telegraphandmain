Employii

An online tool that processes payroll, reduces HR costs, lowers legal risk, and improves compliance.
Translation = Save time and resources on everyday payroll and human resource.


Lower Administration Costs

Reduces the administrative burden of HR and payroll by connecting payroll to other HR functions, like onboarding, benefits, and personnel files so that they talk to each other. Also, automates key HR activities, like data entry, document creation, and compliance to save time and money. Secure, personalized employee webpages drastically cut down on the number of employee requests to the HR administrator.

Boost Productivity & Trim Turnover

The system improves the employee experience in ways that boost productivity and lower turnover. It allows employees to focus on their jobs instead of wasting time on HR activities like onboarding, time-off requests, correcting errors and updating information. In addition to helping you make a good impression, the website and mobile app make employee access to information like compensation, PTO, holidays, benefits,and time and attendance quick and easy.

Reduce Legal Risk & Improve Compliance

HR is the front line of defense against legal and regulatory risk. Employii automates compliance and reduces legal risk by building safeguards into HR processes and automatically creating and storing crucial documentation.
