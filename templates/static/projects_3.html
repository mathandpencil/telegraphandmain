Offers an easier way to create and deliver customized marketing emails. Connect your GMAIL. Import and customize contact lists, create customized marketing campaigns and track your results.

Simply upload your contact list in .csv format and the software automatically organizes the contacts and allows you to customize, copy and delete contacts as needed.
