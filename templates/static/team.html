Team

  • Robert Sanderson

    Bob is majority owner of Candlelight Cabinetry in Lockport, NY (He also started Kitchen World which his son Randy now owns and operates). Bob has extensive entrepreneurial experience; from cornering the hot chocolate market in Medina when he was 8 years old to creating one of the countries highest quality cabinetry lines, he truly has an expert understanding of business startup and development. He was born and raised in Medina and has been a cornerstone in the business and social community for years. Bob has always been passionate about downtown Medina and is consistently enthusiastic about the ongoing preservation and restoration of it's historic buildings.

  • Timothy Hungerford

    Tim is the Chief Design Officer at SocialQ, Employii and Dukemail as well as a co-owner of the software development firm Math & Pencil. He's been working in the startup world for several years now and was also born and raised in Medina. He has lived all across the country and traveled extensively throughout the world working on farms, climbing, surfing, drinking wine and enjoying the vast arrays of cultural traditions and architectural beauty our world has to offer. He's recently moved back to WNY with his lovely wife Teresa, where they are hoping to get heavily involved in the preservation and redevelopment of local architecture.

  • Adam Sokol Architecture Practice, PLLC

    asap is a collaborative design practice founded in 2007 in Buffalo, New York which seeks to identify and advance design opportunities to develop the art and craft of design at all scales -- from tableware to entire cities. Their work is devoted to understanding and contributing to existing cultural and architectural contexts while deploying advanced design and construction technology in ways appropriate to contemporary life. Needless to say... We're super excited to have them working with us on this project.
